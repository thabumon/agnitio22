About The Event

The Department of Computer science and engineering in collaboration with Student association MATRICS of MES College of Engineering, Kuttippuram is organizing the signature event AGNITIO'22 that provides a platform for students to tap their potential and unravel their quiescent talents. Students from various backgrounds and cultures will get an opportunity to interact and appreciate one and other. It will bestow students with an unforgettable learning experience as AGNITIO'22 offers a whole gamut of events.

  • Shall transport facility be provided by the college?

    No, There will not be transportation facilities/ arrangements from the side of college.

  • Is there a clock room arranged for the participants on the first day of the event?

    Yes, Clock room will be provided for the participants for your baggage.

  • Is it possible for one participant to attend more than one workshop?

    No, one participant can only attend one workshop due to the scheduling of time.
